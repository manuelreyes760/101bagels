How we Started lol 

Located just minutes from the Oceanside Pier, 101 Bagels and Subs has been serving up fresh, made-from-scratch bagels and hearty sandwiches since 2012. What started as a small family-run café along historic Highway 101 quickly became a local favorite for breakfast, lunch, and everything in between. Every morning, our bakers hand-roll and boil our bagels the traditional way before baking them to golden perfection. From classic New York–style bagels to our signature California-inspired sandwiches, we take pride in crafting food that’s as authentic as it is delicious.

At 101 Bagels and Subs, we’re all about community, quality, and flavor. Our ingredients are sourced from local California farms and trusted regional suppliers, ensuring every bite is fresh and full of flavor. Whether you’re stopping by for a morning coffee and bagel on your way to the beach or grabbing a loaded sub after a surf session, our Oceanside shop offers a welcoming spot to relax and refuel. Come taste why locals call us the best bagel and sandwich shop on the coast!
